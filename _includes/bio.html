Short Bio

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at IBM Research Zurich, in the Cloud and Computing Infrastructure Department (Industry Platforms and Blockchain Group), where I am working on the Hyperledger Fabric project. Previously, I obtained a PhD from EPFL, in Lausanne, Switzerland, under the supervision of Prof. Rachid Guerraoui. During my PhD, I also spent time with VMware Research and Microsoft Research. I was a recipient of a Best Paper Award at the ACM Middleware Conference in 2014, and of a VMware academic fellowship in 2015-2016.
